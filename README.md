# How QuestLife Works

**QuestLife** is an **RPG-inspired productivity app** that transforms your daily habits and tasks (called **Quests**) into a **character progression system**.

Every positive action you take in real life helps your **digital avatar grow stronger**. Instead of simply checking off tasks, you **level up your character while improving your life**.

# Core Mechanics

## Level & XP (Experience Points)

Your **Level** represents your overall progress in the game.

You gain levels by accumulating **XP (Experience Points)**.

### How to Earn XP
You earn XP by completing:

- **Habits**
- **Quests**

### XP Rewards

The amount of XP you receive depends on the **difficulty** of the habit or quest:

| Difficulty | Reward |
|------------|--------|
| Trivial | Small XP reward |
| Easy | Medium XP reward |
| Hard | Large XP reward |

### Progression Curve

As you reach higher levels, the XP required to reach the next level **gradually increases**.

QuestLife follows an **exponential progression curve**, ensuring that the game remains **challenging and rewarding** as you advance.

---

## Character Stats

Your character grows stronger as you improve different areas of your life.

There are **five main attributes**:

| Stat | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| ❤️ **HP (Health)** | Automatically increases as your character levels up |
| ⚔️ **ATK (Attack)** | Boosted by completing **Productivity** habits |
| 🛡️ **DEF (Defense)** | Boosted by **Health & Fitness** habits |
| 🔮 **MANA** | Boosted by **Creative and Learning** habits |
| 🍀 **LUCK** | Boosted by **Social** habits |

Each habit you complete contributes to improving **specific character attributes**, reinforcing positive real-life behaviors.

---

## Equipment System

The **Equipment Screen** allows you to customize your avatar and gain **powerful stat bonuses**.

### Equipment Slots

Your character can equip items in **four different slots**:

- 🪖 Helmet  
- 🛡️ Armor  
- ⚔️ Weapon  
- 💍 Accessory  

### Unlocking Gear

Equipment is **locked behind level milestones**.

As you level up, new gear automatically becomes available.

Example milestones:

- Level 5
- Level 10
- Level 20

### Stat Bonuses

Each piece of equipment provides a **unique stat boost**.

Example:

- **Strong Sword** → `+10 ATK`
- **Mystic Ring** → `+8 MANA`

### Strategy

You can **mix and match equipment** to specialize your character.

Examples:

- **MANA build** → Better focus and creativity
- **DEF build** → Improve physical health habits
- **ATK build** → Maximize productivity

Choose your equipment wisely to shape the **playstyle of your life RPG**.
